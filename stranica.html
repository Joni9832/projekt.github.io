Welcome to our One Rep Max Calculator!

Curious about your strength and wondering how much you can lift for a single repetition? You've come to the right place!

The One Rep Max (1RM) is the maximum amount of weight you can lift for a single repetition of a given exercise. It's a crucial metric for strength training and is often used to tailor workout routines and set training goals.

Our One Rep Max Calculator simplifies the process of determining your 1RM. Just input the weight and the number of reps you've successfully completed, and we'll calculate your estimated One Rep Max using the Epley formula.

Whether you're a seasoned lifter or just starting your fitness journey, understanding your One Rep Max can help you plan your workouts effectively and track your progress over time.
